Prove Your Knowledge: Key Certifications

Certifications are how you prove to employers that you have a foundational level of knowledge. For entry-level roles, these are the two most important names to know.

CompTIA Security+

This is the **gold standard entry-level certification**. It covers the fundamental principles of network security and risk management. It's a multiple-choice exam, but it is globally recognized and respected by virtually all employers as the baseline for a security professional.

EC-Council CEH

The Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) is one of the most well-known certifications, famous for getting your resume past HR filters, especially for government and corporate jobs. While it started as a multiple-choice exam, the **CEH (Practical)** is now available to prove your hands-on skills.

The Golden Combination

Use platforms like **TryHackMe** to build real, practical skills. Then, use that foundational knowledge to study for and pass your **Security+** exam. This combination of proven hands-on ability and a recognized certification makes you an incredibly strong candidate for your first job.
